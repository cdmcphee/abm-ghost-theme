Paragraph & Inline Formatting

This is a standard paragraph in Crimson Pro at 1.16rem. It should feel like reading a well-typeset book — warm, unhurried, the kind of text you settle into. The line-height is 1.72, generous enough for the eye to rest between lines without losing its place.

This paragraph has bold text, italic text, struck-through text, and a hyperlink to somewhere. There's also inline code for when you need to reference a variable or a file-name.md in the middle of prose.

What does "the divergent mind needs convergent systems" mean?

Divergent minds generate constantly — ideas, connections, possibilities. Without convergent systems to catch, sort, and surface that output, the energy dissipates. Building the systems IS the creative work.

Why is this called A Broad Margin?

From Thoreau's Walden: "I love a broad margin to my life." Thoreau meant leaving room for the unplanned — space to think, wander, notice.

}
